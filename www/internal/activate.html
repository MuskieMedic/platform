Custom domain

If you have a domain you own, make sure you have correct records on your DNS server:
A [Device IP] example.com
CNAME *.example.com example.com
If you do not have a DNS server and want to try on your LAN, edit your hosts file::
[Device IP] example.com (device itself)
[Device IP] [app].example.com (line per app)

Syncloud account

You can use free Syncloud name service (DNS) to get a device name at syncloud.it:
You need to register at syncloud.it to control one or more device names.
Syncloud account is also used for notifications about new releases.
It is used only to assing a dns name to IP of your device and update IP when it changes. Data transfer happens directly between your apps and device.

Device credentials

Device credentials are used to access your device and all the apps (as admin user). They are stored on device and no one knows them. If you forget them you will need to reactivate your device.
